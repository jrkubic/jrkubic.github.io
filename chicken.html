How to take care of Chicken

Chicken is a very special cat. Here is how to properly care for her:

  • Litterbox in the mudroom by the back door. Please clean every visit. Small trash bags are nearby in the mudroom for disposal. They can be put in a sealable bin or our dumpster.
  • Water bowls are near the back door. Please ensure she has clean water available.
  • The food is on an auto timer. No need to mess with it. It pours itself at 7am and 8pm.
  • Her treats are located in the kitchen.
  • There are plants in the living room and kitchen that may need watering.
  • There may be mail at the front door that could be brought in as well.
